What is operator?

Operator is a special symbol that tells the compiler to perfrom some specific task.

What is operand?

In which we can apply operator that's called operand.

What is expression?

combination of operator and operand we can make a expression.

Types of operator:-

Based on the operand we can divide in three category.

1.Uniary operator=>

It's the operator which is applicable on one operand only.

Syntax:-

operand++

example, increament(++) and decreament(--) operator

2.Binary operator=>

It's the operator which is applicable on two operand.

Syntax:-

operand + operand

example=>

arithmetic operator
logical operator

3.Ternary operator

It's applicable on three operand.

conditional operator.
