Arithmetic operator

To perfrom some arithmetic operation with operand,we use arithmetic operator.Arithmetic operator is a binary operator,means It can take two operand and perfrom the operation.

List of arithmetic operator available in javascript.

1.Addition(+)
2.Substraction(-)
3.Multiplication(*)
4.Division(/)
5.exponential(**) <introduced in ES6>
6.modulus(%) <you get reminder by using modulo operator>
